👤 New-in-Town User Flow
Scenario: Priya just moved to Vijayawada. She wants Organic Milk within 5km — cheapest possible.
1
Opens NearBuy
Browser prompts for location. Priya taps "Allow". App detects Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh. No sign-up required.
2
Types "Organic Milk" in search
Autocomplete suggests "Organic Milk 500ml", "Organic Milk 1L". She picks 1L. Radius is defaulted to 5km.
3
Map loads with 6 stores
Pins appear color-coded: green = cheapest, yellow = mid, red = expensive. "BEST PRICE" badge highlights Nature's Best supermarket at ₹58.
4
Taps the green pin
Comparison card expands: shows ₹58, 1.2km away, 4.3⭐, In Stock (12 units). She sees she'd save ₹31 vs the expensive store nearby.
5
Taps "Get Directions"
Opens native maps app with turn-by-turn navigation. ETA: 4 min walk. Mission accomplished in under 60 seconds total.
6
Post-visit: Price Verification
App sends a nudge: "Did you find Organic Milk at ₹58?" Priya confirms with one tap — helping the community stay accurate.
